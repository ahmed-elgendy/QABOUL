Istanbul Medipol University is one of the best Turkish universities and the best universities in Europe. The university is located in a place surrounded by a rich cultural heritage, where the continents of Asia and Europe meet. Istanbul Medipol University has two campuses in Kawacik and Halic district, which includes spacious halls equipped with the latest audio-visual equipment, tennis and basketball courts, recreational areas for student gatherings and nearly 50 student clubs, as well as cinema, theater and photography studios. Halic Campus is located on the European side of Istanbul and houses the faculties of medicine and dentistry. The Kawajik Campus is located on the Asian side of the city and houses the faculties of pharmacy, health sciences, engineering, social sciences, literature and the arts. Istanbul Medipol University is famous for its active role in health fields, as the university has a unique educational complex, which includes hospitals for human medicine and dentistry, and offers university students many opportunities in applied education. Besides the other health facilities of the university, which are the Dental Hospital on the Halic Campus, Vatan Health Center, Fındıkzade Hospital and the Center Esenler Health University vision To be a leading university at the global level that provides scientific results that benefit science and society. University mission Graduating students who can contribute to the production of science and technology, who can respond to the ever-changing demands of society and who bear the responsibility of contributing to scientific knowledge as well as to the development of society.
