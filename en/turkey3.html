Sabanci University

With science, technology and social structures advancing day by day, the world needs more qualified individuals who possess the knowledge and skills required for the labor market anywhere in the world. Recognizing this important issue, the Sabanci Group decided in 1994 to establish a “global university” under its leadership. In the summer of 1995, the most comprehensive social responsibility project in the field of education for Sabancı University was launched. In a research conference attended by 50 academics from 22 countries as well as students and representatives from the private sector, the conference was just the beginning, marking the beginning of a wide-ranging process during which leading educational institutions in Turkey and around the world were examined to see how they operate and to identify the fundamentals that made them successful. . By studying the academic system and work system in the best educational institutions in the world, Sabanci University was designed as a new and unique university. Since 1999, when students started enrolling, Sabanci University has set itself a model for many universities. Confessions of Sabanci University
