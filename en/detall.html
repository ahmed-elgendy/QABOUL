Drawing on experience spanning more than 60 years, Istanbul Kultur University was established by Kultur Education Foundation (KEV) one of the best and oldest private higher education institutions in Turkey. Istanbul Kultur University is a university that provides academic education in accordance with contemporary national and global values ​​and international standards. The university offers academic and scientific activities through 59 departments and programs in eight faculties and two vocational schools. In addition, the university offers about 54 master’s programs and 11 doctoral programs Students at Istanbul Kultur University can work part-time in different departments and units within the university in jobs that correspond to their interests and abilities and have the opportunity to develop their personalities and skills and obtain professional experience, which contributes to graduating productive individuals with knowledge and knowledge of the skills required in different jobs. The university also has a center to qualify students and provide them with the necessary experiences in working life. The center works on developing students and graduates by helping them discover themselves in choosing jobs and professions that are compatible with their interests, skills, abilities, goals and values.
